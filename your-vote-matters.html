Why Your Vote Matters

We express our beliefs in many ways, but one of the most effective is voting. Voting for officials that hold your same values can change the trajectory of our education, our neighborhoods, our finances and our lives and futures.

Whether it’s at the municipal, state or federal level, your voice matters. Elections matter. Your voice is heard by your vote. A single vote can decide an election. Use your voice through your vote to send a powerful message about what you care about and who you want representing your interests. She Votes Illinois is here to make sure that you have all the tools you need to vote!
